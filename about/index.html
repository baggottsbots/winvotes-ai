Who We Are

WinVotes.ai is a Beaufort County, South Carolina election digital marketing firm that builds and runs full digital operations for candidates in local, state, and national races. We are based in Bluffton and serve campaigns across the Lowcountry, throughout South Carolina, and in all 50 states.

We are not a large agency with account managers you'll never meet. Every account is run personally by our founder, Ryan Baggott. That means fast decisions, direct communication, and a campaign operator who treats your race like his own.

What We Build and Run

WinVotes.ai handles the full digital operation so candidates can focus on voters. Our services include:

  • Campaign websites — built, deployed, and live on your domain in under 24 hours
  • AI voter agents — trained on your platform, answering voter questions around the clock
  • Social media management — AI-generated graphics, video, and copy on a consistent schedule
  • SMS and AI voice outreach — text blasts, GOTV pushes, and outbound voice campaigns
  • Endorsement and event pages — live-updated and shareable
  • Real-time lead CRM — every supporter captured, every lead followed up
  • Paid social media advertising — targeted Facebook and Instagram ads built for local races

Our Approach

Local races are decided by margins. Margins are built by consistent execution — never missing a post day, never dropping a lead, never going dark when voters are paying attention. WinVotes.ai runs a system, not a hope. AI tools handle the volume. Ryan handles the strategy and stays in direct contact with every campaign.
